## 2. New Apple distribution permission

Vadim Bulavintsev grants the permission in sections 2 through 7 for all
copyrightable Tentura material he owns or is authorized to license. Each
contributor accepting Tentura CLA version 1.1, or a later CLA version that
expressly grants this exception, grants the same permission for that
contributor's Contribution. Any other copyright holder may adopt it expressly
for material that holder identifies.

In this exception:

- `Covered Licence` means GPLv3 or AGPLv3 as applicable to the material.
- `Apple Materials` means unmodified Apple-provided SDKs, frameworks,
  libraries, APIs, runtime components, toolchains, signing services, and
  platform components for iOS, iPadOS, macOS, watchOS, tvOS, or visionOS that
  are governed by Apple's terms and are not part of Tentura's Corresponding
  Source.
- `App Store` means an Apple-operated software distribution service.
- `Covered App` means a Tentura executable, including a modified version, that
  contains or is combined with material under a Covered Licence.

Notwithstanding conditions of the Covered Licence that would otherwise forbid
the following solely because of Apple-imposed terms or technical measures, you
may:

1. link or combine covered Tentura material with Apple Materials and reproduce
   and convey the resulting Covered App;
2. submit and convey the Covered App through the App Store; and
3. authorize Apple, only as necessary for App Store distribution, to host,
   reproduce, review, code-sign, notarize, encrypt, apply digital-rights or
   installation controls to, transmit, and deliver the Covered App.

This permission excuses a conflict caused solely and unavoidably by Apple's
standard store agreement, code signing, encryption, digital-rights management,
device installation controls, or other technical distribution conditions. It
does not excuse restrictions selected or added by the app distributor and it
does not extend to a distribution channel other than an App Store.

## 3. Corresponding Source

No later than the first App Store availability of a Covered App, its complete
Corresponding Source must be available at no charge from a publicly accessible
network server without an Apple account, App Store account, password, device
restriction, encryption, digital-rights management, or acceptance of terms
that contradict the Covered Licence.

The Corresponding Source must match the exact App Store version and include all
material required by the applicable Covered Licence, including controlled
build scripts and non-secret build and configuration material. It must remain
available for as long as that version is distributed through the App Store and
for at least three years after its last App Store distribution.

The Covered App must contain, in an About, Legal, Source, or similarly
prominent screen, a working source URL and the applicable Covered Licence and
exception notices. The App Store listing must also include the source URL when
Apple provides a field in which it can reasonably be placed.

## 4. Installation information and Apple-controlled credentials

You must provide Installation Information required by the Covered Licence that
you possess or control. You need not provide an Apple-controlled signing key,
certificate, entitlement, notarization credential, or other installation
information that you neither possess nor can lawfully convey. This paragraph
excuses only that Apple-controlled inability; it does not excuse withholding
keys, scripts, entitlements, or installation information that you control.

## 5. Licence and downstream rights

Except for the narrow permission above, the Covered App and its Corresponding
Source remain governed by their Covered Licences. The permission does not
convert AGPL-covered or GPL-covered material to a proprietary licence, does not
restrict source recipients from exercising their Covered Licence rights
outside Apple's systems, and does not authorize additional restrictions on
non-Apple distribution.

You must preserve this exception and all applicable copyright, licence,
authorship, modification, warranty, and project-provenance notices in the
Corresponding Source and in the Covered App's Appropriate Legal Notices.

## 6. AGPL network interaction

For AGPL-covered material and a GPLv3/AGPLv3 combination, this exception does
not waive AGPLv3 section 13. If a modified version supports remote network
interaction, every user interacting with it remotely must still receive a
prominent opportunity to obtain the complete Corresponding Source as required
by AGPLv3.

## 7. Scope and severability

This exception grants no trademark rights, rights in Apple Materials, rights
to App Store data, or patent rights beyond those supplied by the applicable
Covered Licence or CLA. A copyright holder grants it only for material that
holder owns or is authorized to license.

If a condition of this exception is not satisfied, the exception supplies no
permission for the affected conveyance; rights otherwise available under the
Covered Licence are unaffected. If part of the exception is unenforceable, the
remaining parts continue to the extent allowed by applicable law.
