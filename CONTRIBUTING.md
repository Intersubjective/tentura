# Contributing to Tentura

Tentura accepts contributions under the GNU Affero General Public License,
version 3 only (`AGPL-3.0-only`). Do not submit work that cannot be distributed
on those terms.

Before a contribution can be accepted, every individual contributor must
accept [CLA.md](CLA.md). If a contribution is made on behalf of an employer or
another rights holder, a person authorized to bind that rights holder must
also accept the agreement.

## Acceptance procedure

Until an automated CLA service is configured, include the following statement
in the pull-request description or an authenticated pull-request comment,
together with your legal name:

> I have read and accept the Tentura Individual Contributor License Agreement
> version 1.1 dated 19 July 2026. I have authority to submit this contribution
> on its terms.

The maintainer must retain the acceptance record and link it to the merged
commit. A `Signed-off-by` line or GitHub's default inbound-equals-outbound rule
does not replace the CLA acceptance required by this project.

## Contribution requirements

- Preserve applicable license, copyright, provenance, and third-party notices.
- Identify copied, generated, or third-party material and its source and
  license.
- Do not identify an AI system as an author. Disclose materially AI-generated
  submissions so their provenance and provider terms can be reviewed.
- Keep your real authorship attribution. The project-stewardship notice in
  [NOTICE](NOTICE) is separate from individual authorship.
- Add `SPDX-License-Identifier: AGPL-3.0-only` to a new source file where the
  file format permits comments, unless a different reviewed notice is
  required.

The project may reject a contribution when its ownership or licensing cannot
be established.
